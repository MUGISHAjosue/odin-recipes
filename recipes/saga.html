This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make

with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Prepare pastry: Combine flour, curry powder, and salt in a bowl. Cut in margarine and shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs
  3. Prepare filling: Melt margarine in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion until soft and translucent, 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Spoon equal amounts of filling onto each pastry circle. Fold over and press edges together, making a half-circle.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
